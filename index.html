About Us

The NY Ovals Cricket Summer Camp is a fun, structured, and professionally coached program designed to help young athletes learn and improve their cricket skills.

Our mission is to teach cricket fundamentals, build confidence, and inspire a lifelong love for the sport.

Camp Details

The NY Ovals Summer Camp is a 4‑week program designed to build cricket fundamentals and confidence.

Who Can Participate?

  • 7–9 years: Basic cricket introduction
  • 10–12 years: Skill development
  • 13+ years: Advanced training

Schedule

  • 4 weeks
  • Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
  • 3 hours per session
